Malala Yousafzai

The girl who stood up for education

Malala Yousafazai is a Pakistani activist for female education. She won the Nobel Peace Prize at 17.

A Brief Timeline of Malala's life:

  • 1997 - Malala is born
  • 2008 - Malala's first speech
  • 2009 - Malala begins to blog for the BBC under the name Gul Makai. The Taliban comes to the Swat valley and Malala's identity is slowly revealed.
  • 2011- Wins Pakistan's National Youth Peace Prize
  • 2012 - The Taliban shoot Malala as she rides home from school. Two other girls are wounded. A Fatwa is issued against the gunman.
  • 2013 - Malala speaks at the UN and releases her book.
  • 2014 - Malala wins the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2017 - 5 years after the shooting, Malala begins her studies at Oxford.

One child, one teacher, one book, one pen can change the world.

-Malala Yousafzai
